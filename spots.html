Must-Visit Landmarks

The iconic monuments that define the skyline of Delhi.

Tourist Attractions

From war memorials to ancient minarets, these are the places you cannot miss.

India Gate

A war memorial located astride the Rajpath. It is a tribute to the soldiers of the British Indian Army.

Red Fort

The historic fort in Old Delhi that served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors.

Lotus Temple

A Baháʼí House of Worship known for its flower-like shape. It is open to all, regardless of religion.
