The City of Djinns

Delhi is a city that bridges two worlds. Old Delhi, once the capital of the Mughals, is a labyrinth of narrow lanes, ancient havelis, and formidable mosques. In contrast, New Delhi is a city of wide boulevards, imposing government buildings, and lush green parks.
