The Food Capital

From spicy Chole Bhature to rich Butter Chicken, taste the soul of Delhi.

A Culinary Journey

Delhi's food culture is a mix of Mughal influence, Punjabi spices, and modern fusion. Whether you are standing at a street stall in Old Delhi or dining in a cafe at Khan Market, the flavors here are unforgettable.

Street Food

Must Try: Chole Bhature at Sitaram Diwan Chand and Golgappe at Bengali Market.

Mughlai Delights

Must Try: Kebabs at Karim's and Butter Chicken at Moti Mahal (the place where it was invented!).

Desserts

Must Try: Jalebis at Old Famous Jalebi Wala and Kulfi at Karol Bagh.
